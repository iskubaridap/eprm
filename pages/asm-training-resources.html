ASM - Training Resources

Welcome to the ASM Training and Resources page. Here you will find multiple ways to learn ASM. You should also have a designated Super User who can help you through the process. If you are unsure who this is, ask your supervisor. The system will get easier as you become more familiar with it.

Also, you can always contact the EPRM Help desk at erpmhelp@hii-tsd.com.

After viewing the videos, the recommended plan for training is:

  1. ASM Process Overview
  2. ASM Computer-based Training (CBT) Lessons
  3. Job Aids to Navigate Each Step
  4. ASM User Guide with images, separated by Chapter
  5. Visit the ASM Help site's Communications page often, for important updates on changes in the ASM tool

NOTE: Sometimes the training material is not up to date with the ASM tool. Changes to training material may take up to one month from the time the software changes take affect.
