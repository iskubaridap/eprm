Registration Instructions:

EPRM is available to all DoD users who have a SIPRNET email account and need access to conduct inspections/assessments. Non-DOD personnel should reach out with questions.

For EPRM access, please email the following required information to raleigh.onks.ctr@us.af.mil AND Caleb.L.Jones.ctr@mail.mil:

  • Name
  • Title / Rank
  • Phone Number (NOT DSN)
  • Service or Component
  • Major Command
  • Installation / Base / Fort
  • Unit
  • NIPR E-Mail
  • SIPR E-Mail
  • Assessment Type (OPSEC, IP, ATFP, IDRMP, DoD Insider, ASM, PKI, Mission Assurance -- access to Mission Assurance is limited to DTRA and Combatant Command MAA Teams)
  • For ASM users, please include the following:
    • Center (i.e., AFLNWC)
    • Two letter Directorate (i.e., ND)
    • Three letter Program (i.e., ND1)
    • Program Executive Officer (PEO)
