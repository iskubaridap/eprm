How to request in-person training:

For MARMS (ATFP or Mission Assurance)

Send the request to bryan.b.driskell.civ@mail.mil
&CC ryburn.p.ross.ctr@mail.mil

Include this information

  • Name
  • Title / Rank
  • Phone Number (NOT DSN)
  • Service or Component
  • Who will be the audience for training
  • What type of training is required: User, Higher HQ, Train the Trainer
  • How many people would you expect to attend
  • What are your desired dates for the one-day training (list 3)
  • Do you want the training team to come to you or you come to them in Alexandria, VA
  • Are you willing to fund the training
  • Additional Information/Background
