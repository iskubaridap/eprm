The Information Protection Module in EPRM

Comprised of Information, Personnel, and Industrial Security countermeasures

Why

The purpose of the module is to automate Information Protection assessments and allow senior leaders to receive trend analysis. The module consists of 63 Information Security questions, 21 Personnel Security questions, 25 Industrial Security questions, and 23 CUI questions.

Policy

AFI 16-1404 formerly mandated AF unit information personnel and security assessments to be conducted using EPRM.

Information Protection self-inspections are required to be conducted annually by the end of the fiscal year in accordance with DoD Memo 5200.01. Use of EPRM is optional and allows users to customize their assessments for their unit. For example, if a unit does not have any aircraft assets, the unit will not need to address countermeasure questions regarding aircraft assets. This saves time by allowing the users to focus on items pertaining to their unit and eliminates the need to have 'N/A' responses.

The IP module in EPRM was built using these regulations:

  • DoDM 5200.01, Volume 1, AFM 16-1404 Volume 1, 11 JAN 2021
  • DoDM 5200.01, Volume 2, AFM 16-1404 Volume 2, 07 JAN 2021
  • DoDM 5200.01, Volume 3, AFM 16-1404 Volume 1, 23 DEC 2020
  • DoDM 5220.22, Volume 2, AFM 16-1406 Volume 2, 08 MAY 2020
